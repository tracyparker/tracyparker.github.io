FRACTALS

Fractals are everywhere. Trees are fractal, snowflakes are fractal, fractal configurations are used in electronics...our world is filled with fractals. Yet most people couldn't tell you what a fractal is. My journey of fractal discovery has opened doors for me as an artist that I never thought possible.
