Welcome!

Hi there fellow art lovers! Welcome to my site! Thank you for taking the time out of your day to let me share my work with you!

While you're here, I hope you find images that inspire you, that cause you to be curious about the world we live in. Images that make you tilt your head to the side like a dog and say "What is that?!". Images that speak to your heart.

I hope you enjoy your experience on my site. I have my work broken down into five sections. Please keep in mind as you click around, that the site is my work as well. I'm planning to eventually add a sixth section to this site to showcase my web design and coding skills.

Feel free to contact me with any questions/comments about my work, or any problems you have with the site itself.

Thanks!

Tracy Parker

PHOTOGRAPHY

People, places, things. Some studio work from my college days. Nature shots. Politicians. Protests. A mix of this and that.

FRACTALS

Fractals are everywhere. Trees are fractal, snowflakes are fractal, fractal configurations are used in electronics...our world is filled with fractals. Yet most people couldn't tell you what a fractal is. My journey of fractal discovery has opened doors for me as an artist that I never thought possible.
